DOCUMENT MANAGEMNT SYSTEM

Easy-to-Use, Real-time System Gives Clients Peace of Mind

We offer a state-of-the-art document management system for our clients, linguists and internal team members. Clients can provide detailed project instructions and upload files of all sizes and types, including Word, PDF, text, audio and video files, in seconds. The internal team is notified immediately that files are available for review and projects are assigned to a dedicated team of linguists.

Once the documents are translated by our linguists, they are reviewed by our quality control team who then issues an notarized affidavit certifying accuracy. At every stage, clients receive real-time status updates. Once the project is completed, clients can download the files and do a side-by-side comparison of the original and translated content.

Clients have peace of mind using our document management system.
