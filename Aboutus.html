COMPANY OVERVIEW

Strong Foundation, Exceptional Experience

Quality Translations was built on a foundation of many years of servicing the legal and financial
industries across a broad range of document-based services. Quality Translation is the sister
company of two industry leaders—Diamond Personnel and Quality EDGAR Solutions—
and has unique experience in the staffing of large translation projects, as well as expertise in
document production and technology.

Diamond Personnel has been staffing legal document review and translation projects for more
than 21 years. Quality EDGAR Solutions has been servicing the document production and
regulatory filing needs of a broad range of public companies for more than a decade.

Quality Translations has taken these capabilities and built a suite of services that is unmatched
in the industry.

CUSTOMER SERVICE & QUALITY ASSURANCE

Top Quality Translators Available Around the Clock

Our service model has been designed to accommodate high-volume, urgent requests at any time. With a full-
service production hub in New York and translators working across all time zones, we are fully operational
24/7/365. Our quality control begins with a global recruiting commitment. We identify elite translators (linguists),
vet and test them, and approve only the very best: currently, we qualify less than 5% of our applicant pool.

To ensure consistent excellence from our translators, we provide real-time feedback on their work from other
expert translators. Our quality-assurance team also reconciles any discrepancies in a given translation before it
is returned to the client.

We work to increase accuracy by applying glossaries and style guidelines, and building and maintaining translation
teams for long-term and/or ongoing projects.
